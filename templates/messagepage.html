Our Purpose

Enhance user safety through an alert system using an ultrasonic motion detector. Provide users with a reliable and efficient means of detecting motion in their surroundings. Utilize ultrasonic motion sensor technology to effectively detect movement. Promptly notify users when motion is detected. Improve user's safety and provide peace of mind in various environments, including homes, offices, and other relevant settings.

How it works

We are using a ultrasonic motion sensor that is connected to a raspberry pi in order to see motion detected.
